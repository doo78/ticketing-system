About University Helpdesk

Our Mission

University Helpdesk is created by students, for students. We understand the unique technical challenges that students face because we experience them too. Our mission is to provide peer-to-peer technical support that's approachable, effective, and tailored to the student experience.

Who We Are

Our team consists entirely of student volunteers from various disciplines across campus. We're tech enthusiasts who love helping our fellow students overcome technical hurdles. Being students ourselves, we understand the time constraints and unique needs of the university community.

Our Values

Peer-to-peer support and understanding. Creating a judgment-free zone for all tech questions. Knowledge sharing and skill development. Flexible support that works around student schedules. Building community through technology.

By Students, For Students

What makes our helpdesk unique is that we speak your language. We've sat in the same classrooms, used the same online platforms, and faced the same technical challenges. We're not just solving your tech problems—we're sharing solutions from one student to another.

Our volunteer hours accommodate student schedules, with extended support during midterms and finals. We're accessible through drop-in sessions at our campus locations, direct messaging on the university app, and our student-monitored email system.

Join Our Team

Are you tech-savvy and enjoy helping others? The University Helpdesk is always looking for student volunteers! Joining our team is a great way to build your resume, develop technical skills, and connect with peers across campus. No advanced technical knowledge required—just a willingness to learn and help fellow students.
